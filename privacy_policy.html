Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/28/2024

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share personal data from Discord accounts. Minimal data (e.g., Discord User IDs, public server messages) may be temporarily processed solely to provide core functionality.

2. How Data is Used

Any data accessed or processed is used exclusively for:

  • Delivering app features (e.g., responding to commands).
  • Improving the App’s performance and functionality.

The App does not use data for analytics, advertising, or other external purposes.

3. Data Retention

No personal data is stored permanently. Any temporary data is deleted immediately after processing.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not share user data with third-party services or external entities.

5. User Responsibility

Users must not share sensitive or private information while interacting with the App. The App is designed to work within Discord’s privacy policies and guidelines.

6. Security

The App uses best practices to safeguard data while it is being processed. However, no system is completely secure, and Pelz cannot guarantee the absolute security of data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to the App, please contact us for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact ayuzon.forgames@gmail.com.
